Preparation Tips

For Engineering Aspirants

  • Focus on NCERT books for basic concepts
  • Practice numerical problems regularly
  • Solve previous year JEE papers
  • Join coaching or online courses if needed
  • Focus on time management during exam preparation

For Medical Aspirants

  • Strong foundation in NCERT Biology
  • Regular practice of diagrams
  • Focus on NEET pattern questions
  • Study anatomy and physiology thoroughly
  • Practice MCQs daily
